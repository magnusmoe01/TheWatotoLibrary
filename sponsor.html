Sponsor A Child

It costs $30 a month to sponsor a child in Kibera. This equals to only $1 per day.

Your contribution goes to the child you are sponsoring and their family in full.
It covers money for school tuition, food

Our childen in need of sponsorships are either among the poorest in Kibera, or they stand out in their given field. See the children below or read more.


About The Sponsorship

    You become the sponsor of a child in Kibera, who you will get a picture of, and who you can contact. We will also send you updates about the child every 6 months, and you will be able to see that your help gives results.

    All new sponsors receive a sponsor information booklet, sponsorship contract and a picture of the sponsored child. In addition, you will receive general information about the place where the child lives.

    Sponsors are welcome to write to the child or send a small note if desired, it is greatly appreciated by the children.

The Children

    The children who are approved to receive support are from families that are so poor that they will not have the opportunity to get out of poverty, or they have a special talent in need of financial aid to be able to reach their full potential.

How the money is spent

    We pay the school tuition and buy the food for the family. That ensures the money is used as it is supposed to.

    The monthly $30 is divided into this: $10 school fees, $14 food, $4 library fee, $2 transaction fees.

Gifts to the child

    Many sponsors want to give gifts to the child and its family. If larger gifts are given, such as mattresses, goats etc., we will send a picture when the gift is handed to the child. You will thus have a more personal relationship with the gift you give.

    At the same time, it is important to remember that the sponsor does not give so many gifts to their child as it creates discord among the children. Most children never receive gifts, so it is better to let our helpers distribute a donation evenly, than for a child to receive a lot of gifts.

Payment

    You are a sponsor for as long as you wish, and can end your involvement at any time. As a sponsor, you undertake to pay a fixed monthly amount of $30 per child. This amount can be paid monthly, quarterly, half-yearly or annually. You can pay by sent giro, via online banking, arrange a fixed draft with your bank, or through any of our payment methods.

Terminating Your Sponsorship

    You can cancel your sponsorship at any time. You can send us a notice of termination per e-mail or through our contact form, and we will stop your sponsorship at the end of the month. It is important that we receive a termination notice, so that we can find a new sponsor for the child as quickly as possible, so that it is not left without help and school facilities. The children are thrown out of school when school fees are not paid, so continuity is important.
